С марта 2020 года объявления о проведении внутренних закупок АО «Фонд проблемных кредитов» размещаются на Евразийском электронном портале. Since March 2020, announcements of internal procurement of JSC Problem Loans Fund have been posted on the Eurasian electronic portal. Potential suppliers can find announcements about conducting internal purchases of JSC "Problem Loans Fund" on the Eurasian electronic portal in the tab "Lots" or "Announcements" using the necessary search parameters using the BIN (business identification number) of JSC "Problem Loans Fund."

To participate in the internal procurement of JSC "Problem Loan Fund," potential suppliers must undergo the registration procedure on the Eurasian electronic portal in accordance with the Supplier's Instruction specified in the Instructions tab.
